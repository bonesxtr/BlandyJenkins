    Taster Session

    A taster session which will consist of an hour's introduction to the club. Meet members of the club and try out one of the provided club bows. Have a taste of archery, before you commit to enrollment on a beginners course.

    Location

    Beginner Courses

    The beginners course will consist of approx 6-10 hrs of coaching from a qualified GNAS /Archery GB or WFAA coach.

    Indoor/Outdoor Shooting

    We shoot both indoors and outdoors. From May to September we shoot outdoors and indoors from October to April.
